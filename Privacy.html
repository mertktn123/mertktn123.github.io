Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how KediNet (“Company”, “we”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application (“User”, “you”).

2. Data We Collect

  • Mandatory: Email, encrypted password, username
  • Optional: Profile photo URL
  • Automatic: Usage statistics, crash reports, device identifiers

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Provide and personalize service
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • App performance analysis and improvement

4. Legal Basis

  • Consent (for optional data)
  • Contract performance (registration)
  • Legitimate interests (security, analytics)

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use third-party analytic cookies. You can manage them via your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party data brokers. Only disclose if legally required.

7. Reporting & Enforcement

  • Users may report cat listings (cat name or description) and chat messages for profanity, spam, or other violations.
  • All reports are reviewed by the KediNet moderation team.
  • If a report is found valid, the offending user may receive a temporary or permanent ban.
  • Upon account deletion (by user or by ban), associated profile images and stored data are also removed from our systems.

8. Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, and portability of your data. Contact kedinetapp@gmail.com to exercise.

9. Updates to Policy

We may update this policy without notice; the latest version is shown in-app.
